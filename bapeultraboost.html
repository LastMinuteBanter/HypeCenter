Adidas BAPE Ultraboost

The BAPE Ultraboost sneaker features a PrimeKnit in BAPE’s signature green camo. (BAPE is a popular streetwear brand in Japan). The three stripes branding on the sides of the shoe are covered in a shade of white and the Boost midsole and the outsole, made by using Continental, is in black. The sneaker was released on the 2nd of February 2019, in collaboration with this year’s Super Bowl event. The adidas branding on the left shoe tongue is detailed in gold.
