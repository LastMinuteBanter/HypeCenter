Nike Air Max 1/97 Sean Wotherspoon

The Nike Air Max 1/97 Sean Wotherspoon is a sneaker designed by Sean Wotherspoon himself as he won the Air Max Sneaker design for competing in the RevolutionAIR voting campaign in early 2017. The sneaker features a unique multi-colored corduroy upper build which is placed on the iconic tooling of the Air Max 1.
