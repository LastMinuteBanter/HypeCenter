Vans x Mastermind x KITH

The Kith x Mastermind x Vans Sk8-Hi “Black” is a limited edition collaboration that has given a premium makeover to the classic vans sneaker. Well known and popular New York based retailer, Kith has teamed up with Japanese streetwear label Mastermind and Vans in 2019 for a premium capsule collection, which including this luxe Sk8-Hi. This sneaker features a premium nubuck upper in an all black with embossed “Peace on Earth/World on Fire” text on the side stripes and branding on the ankle below the metal skull and crossbones. More details include waxed rope laces, text molded into the midsoles, and a skeleton graphic on the waffle outsole. This model was released in January of 2019.
